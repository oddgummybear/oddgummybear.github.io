Soccer, known as football or association football in most of the world, is a popular team sport played between two teams of 11 players on a rectangular field. The objective is to score by propelling a round ball into the opposing goal using any body part except hands and arms, with only the goalkeeper allowed to use their hands within a designated area.

American football is a high-intensity, strategic team sport played by two 11-player teams on a 100-yard field with an oval-shaped leather ball. The primary objective is to score points by advancing the ball into the opponent's end zone (touchdown, 6 points) or kicking it through goalposts (field goal, 3 points).
