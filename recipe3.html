Empire Cookies

 
Prep time: 30 mins Bake time: 10 mins Total time: 40 mins Serves: 36 Cookies Author: Recipe adapted from Canadian Living Type: Cookies

Ingredients

 
 

Cookie

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup raspberry jam

Topping

  • 1 cup icing sugar, sifted
  • ¼ teaspoon pure almond extract
  • 1–2 tablespoons hot water
  • 40 cherry pieces

Instructions

  • Prepare baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  • In a stand mixer beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add egg and vanilla.
  • In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to butter mixture in 2 or 3 additions.
  • On a floured work surface, roll dough to approx. 1/8 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter (1½ inches) to cut circles. Place on baking sheet about 1 inch apart.
  • Bake for 8–10 minutes until bottoms are lightly browned. Cool completely.
  • Spread raspberry jam on half of the cookies and top with the remaining cookies.
  • Make the icing: mix icing sugar and almond extract, add hot water gradually until spreadable. Ice each cookie and top with a cherry piece.
  • Let cookies set for approx. 1 hour.
    • Empire Cookies freeze well. Store in an airtight container for up to a month.
