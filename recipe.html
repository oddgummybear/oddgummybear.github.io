Empire Cookies

Prep time: 30 mins
Bake time: 10 mins
Total time: 40 mins
Serves: 36 Cookies
Author: Recipe adapted from Canadian Living
Type: Cookies

Ingredients

Cookie

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅓ cup raspberry jam

Topping

  • 1 cup icing sugar, sifted
  • ¼ teaspoon pure almond extract
  • 1–2 tablespoons hot water
  • 40 cherry pieces

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350˚F.
  2. Prepare baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  3. In a stand mixer beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy, then add egg and vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to butter mixture in 2 or 3 additions.
  5. On a floured work surface, roll dough to approx. 1/8 inch thick. Use a round cookie cutter (1½ inches) to cut circles. Place on baking sheet about 1 inch apart.
  6. Bake for 8–10 minutes until bottoms are lightly browned. Cool completely.
  7. Spread raspberry jam on half of the cookies and top with the remaining cookies.
  8. Make the icing: mix icing sugar and almond extract, add hot water gradually until spreadable. Ice each cookie and top with a cherry piece.
  9. Let cookies set for approx. 1 hour.
Notes:
Empire Cookies freeze well. Store in an airtight container for up to a month.
